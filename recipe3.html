Directions

  1. In a small heavy saucepan, cook the sugar over low heat until caramel forms. Pour into an 8-inch round mold. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, beat the eggs until frothy.
  3. Add the condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla; mix well with the eggs.
  4. Pour the mixture into the carmelized mold. Cook on top of the stove in a bain-marie on medium-high heat, partially covered, for 35 minutes. The flan is done when a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.
  5. Let it cool at room temperature. Refridgerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
  6. To serve, invert the flan on a platter. Garnish on top with fresh berries.
