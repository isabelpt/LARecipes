About Us

This website is a tribute to my grandfather, Edgar, who is from Puerto Rico. We call him Eggy because he loved to bake and made the best flan ever. While most of what I know about him is from stories that my mom shares, he is still with us through the cookbooks that he has passed down. I was very enthusiastic to make this website not only as an excuse to bake more but also as a way for me to enrich my knowledge of Latin American culture. Getting to learn about these countries, their traditions, and their foods has been such a rewarding process. I have loved getting to learn about people through their unique stories that have been shaped by cooking and baking with loved ones. Food is universal and each of these recipes holds such rich culture and memories baked with love. I hope that this website helps you to bake new foods with people who you love.
Have fun!
