Arroz Con Leche
Cuba

Cuban cuisine features a mix of Spanish, Indigenous Taino foods, Caribbean, and African influences and dishes.

Ingredients

Pastry

Directions

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, bring rice, cinnamon sticks and lemon rind to a boil in water. Reduce heat once boiling to low and cover. Simmer for 10 to 15 minutes, or until rice is tender and plump.
  2. Remove lemon rind with a slotted spoon. Over low heat, add evaporated and condensed milk. Stir constantly to thoroughly combine. Add salt and vanilla extract. Continue to cook on low heat for 7-10 minutes, or until the pudding thickens to desired consistency.
  3. Remove from heat and serve immediately. Or, store in the fridge in an air-tight container.
